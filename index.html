The Dream Of Life

Alan Watts

Let's suppose that you were able everynight
To dream any dream you wanted to dream.
Naturally when you began on this adventure of
Dreams you would fulfill all your wishes.
You would have every kind of pleasure that you could concieve.
After several nights you would say ''well, that was pretty great''

But now let's have a dream which isn't under control.
Something is going to happen to me
And i don't know what it is gonna be.
Then you'd get more and more adventurous
And you would make further and further outgambles
As to what you would dream of.
'Til finally you would dream of where you are now.

If you awaken from that illusion
and understand that black implies white
Self implies other
Life implies death...
You can feel yourself.
Not as a stranger in the world
Not as something here on probation
Not as something that has arrived here by fluke
But you can begin to feel your own existence as absolutely fundamental
What you are basically
Deep deep down
Far far in
It's the fabric and structure of existence itself.
